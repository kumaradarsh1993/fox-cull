Built for the first pass.

Folder-first browsing

Point fox-cull at a drive or shoot folder and review files where they already live. No catalog import step.

Fast keyboard triage

Rate with number keys, pick with P, reject with X, clear with U, then filter and sweep when you are ready.

Video-aware culling

Clips get poster frames, scrub previews, playback, and lossless trim/export for quick delivery cuts.

Your originals stay untouched.

No destructive edits

Ratings and labels live in fox-cull's catalog. Original photo and video files are not modified.

Portable catalogs

Per-drive storage keeps catalog, previews, and recycle data with the drive, including external SSD workflows.

Local desktop app

Tauri, Rust, and Svelte keep the app lightweight. No subscription and no cloud library.
